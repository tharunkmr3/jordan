Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: April 14, 2026

If you want to delete your data from Jordon AI, you have the following options:

Option 1: Delete from your account

  1. Log in to your Jordon AI account at app.jordon.ai.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Click Delete Account at the bottom of the page.
  4. This will permanently delete your account, all agents, conversations, contacts, and knowledge base data.

Option 2: Request deletion via email

Send an email to tharunkmr3@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include:

  • Your account email address
  • Your organization name
  • Whether you want a full account deletion or specific data removal

We will process your request within 7 business days and confirm via email once complete.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile and account information
  • All AI agents and their configurations
  • All conversation history and messages
  • All contacts and customer data
  • All knowledge base documents and embeddings
  • Connected channel configurations (Facebook, WhatsApp tokens are revoked)
  • Usage logs and billing history

Facebook / Instagram data

If you connected your Facebook or Instagram account to Jordon AI, deleting your data will also remove all Facebook-related tokens and page connections from our systems. To additionally revoke Jordon AI's access to your Facebook account, visit your Facebook Business Integrations settings.
