What channels do you support?
Phone calls (inbound & outbound), WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, and website chat widget. Set up once — your AI agent works across all of them from one knowledge base.
Which languages are supported?
English, Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Kannada, Bengali, Marathi, Gujarati, Malayalam, and Punjabi — across voice and chat. The AI detects the language automatically.
How long does setup take?
48 hours from when you share your knowledge base. We handle configuration, testing, and deployment across all channels. You just share your FAQs and docs.
Can the AI book meetings and follow up?
Yes. It collects requirements, checks availability, books meetings, sends confirmations, and follows up — across voice, chat, and WhatsApp.
Is this just a basic chatbot?
No. We use the world's best AI models — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and ElevenLabs for voice. Your agent has real conversations, understands context, and sounds human on calls. Nothing like old-school chatbots.
Can we use this for internal teams too?
Absolutely. Many companies use it for employee queries — leave policies, reimbursements, IT tickets. Set it up on WhatsApp or your internal portal. Customer-facing and internal — same platform.
Do we need a tech team to manage this?
No. We handle everything — setup, training, deployment. You just share your FAQs and docs. Built specifically for businesses without IT teams.
What happens when the AI can't answer?
It transfers to your team with full context — what was discussed, customer details, and why it's escalating. Works the same across phone, WhatsApp, Facebook, and web chat.
